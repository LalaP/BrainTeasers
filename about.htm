Uclick is an app project started by a group of Cal Poly Pomona students. We aim to facilitate learning by utilizing smartphone technology. It is easy to use for both teachers and students. Teachers create surveys, polls, quizzes, questionnaires and students answer them and we provide the statistical analysis of each response. Uclick is coming soon to your Android, Follow our Facebook and Twitter for updates on our progress.
